Detailed AI Model Specifications

Technical Architecture & Capabilities

APOLLO - Predictive Optimization Model

Parameters: 20.8M | Architecture: Multi-task LSTM | Size: ~100MB
Master coordinator orchestrating all subsystems. Provides cost prediction, maintenance prioritization across 5 levels, and optimization actions across 7 categories. Real-time inference at 11Hz.

AQUILO - Electrical System Diagnostics

Parameters: 608K | Architecture: Deep NN + Attention | Size: ~20MB
Monitors power quality and detects 13 electrical fault types. Perfect detection of critical faults including overcurrent and phase imbalance. All fault types achieve >90% accuracy.

BOREAS - Refrigeration Analysis

Parameters: 1.2M | Architecture: Deep NN + Self-Attention | Size: ~100MB
Analyzes refrigeration systems detecting 17 fault types. Perfect detection of liquid slugging, low refrigerant, and oil logging. Uses 3-stage progressive fine-tuning for optimal performance.

NAIAD - Water System Monitoring

Parameters: 533K | Architecture: Carefully Balanced NN | Size: ~15MB
Monitors hydraulic systems detecting 16 water fault types. Features advanced leak detection, flow anomaly identification, and comprehensive pump health monitoring with near-perfect accuracy.

VULCAN - Mechanical System Health

Parameters: 476K | Architecture: Multi-task Learning NN | Size: ~18MB
Analyzes rotating equipment with vibration analysis (0.418 RMS). Provides bearing health prediction (90% accuracy), system efficiency monitoring, and perfect detection of bearing failure and misalignment.

ZEPHYRUS - Airflow Optimization

Parameters: 845K | Architecture: Deep NN for Time-Series | Size: ~25MB
Optimizes ventilation detecting 17 airflow fault types. All fault types achieve >95% accuracy. Includes filter degradation analysis and indoor air quality monitoring.

COLOSSUS - Multi-Model Integration

Parameters: 17.3M | Architecture: Multi-head Attention Fusion | Size: ~200MB
Aggregates insights from all specialist models. Performs cross-system correlation, cascade detection, and advanced pattern recognition across domains with perfect accuracy.

GAIA - Safety Validation System

Parameters: 2.4M | Architecture: Safety-Critical Validation NN | Size: ~30MB
Critical safety layer with 5-state classification (SAFE, WARNING, OVERRIDE, EMERGENCY, UNCERTAIN). Zero false negatives in safety-critical scenarios. Validates all actions before execution.

Total Parameters: ~43M across all models | Edge-optimized with INT8 quantization | PyTorch 2.x framework
