Key Findings & Recommendations

✓ Consistent Usage Pattern

The facility shows predictable seasonal usage patterns with summer peaks (195k kWh) and winter lows (100k kWh), averaging 137k kWh monthly over the 4.5-year period.

⚠️ Dramatic Cost Escalation (2025)

Critical Finding: Energy costs have increased 47-69% in 2025 compared to 2021-2024 averages:

  • • 2021-2024 average: $0.114/kWh
  • • 2025 average: $0.169/kWh
  • • Peak 2025 rate: $0.233/kWh (April 2025)
  • • Projected 2025 annual increase: +$135,385 over 2024

💡 Energy Efficiency Priority

Given rising costs, energy efficiency improvements have a significantly higher ROI than in previous years. Projects that reduce consumption by 10% would save approximately $34,700 annually at 2025 rates versus $21,200 at 2024 rates.
